04 — HOW IT WORKS
🧠
The Model
ResNet-50 pretrained on ImageNet — a state-of-the-art image classifier trained on 1.2 million images across 1000 categories.
⚡
FGSM Attack
Computes the gradient of the loss with respect to each pixel, then nudges pixels in the direction that maximally increases the model's error.
🔁
PGD Attack
Projected Gradient Descent — iterates FGSM multiple times with smaller steps, producing a stronger and more targeted perturbation.
🔒
Why It Matters
Adversarial vulnerabilities affect real-world AI systems — from facial recognition to autonomous vehicles. Understanding them is key to building robust AI.
